Account Deletion Overview

We understand that you may no longer wish to use ChemFetch. This page provides clear instructions on how to permanently delete your account and explains what happens to your data during this process.

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to export any data you wish to keep before proceeding.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted:

  • You will lose access to all your chemical inventory data
  • Your scan history and saved products will be permanently deleted
  • Any team or company data associated with your account may be affected
  • You will not be able to recover your account or data
  • Your login credentials will be permanently removed
