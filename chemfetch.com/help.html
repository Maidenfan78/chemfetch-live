1

Getting Started

Join the ChemFetch mobile app closed testing program on Android. Contact support for access to the internal testing version.

  • Currently in closed testing on Android 5.0+ (iOS version coming 2025)
  • Requires camera permission for barcode scanning
  • Works offline - data syncs when connected
  • Access via Google Play Store internal testing program
2

Barcode Scanning

Use the camera to scan product barcodes. The app supports multiple barcode formats and provides instant feedback.

  • Supports EAN-8, EAN-13, UPC-A, UPC-E, ITF-14, Code128/39/93
  • Requires multiple identical scans to prevent errors
  • Haptic feedback confirms successful scans
  • Automatic duplicate detection prevents re-adding items
üì∏ Scanning Tips

Hold your device steady, ensure good lighting, and align the barcode within the white frame. The app will vibrate when a successful scan is detected.

3

Manual Entry

When barcodes can't be scanned or don't exist, manually enter product information including name, size, and barcode (if available).

  • Enter product name and size/weight
  • Add barcode manually if visible but not scannable
  • Validation prevents duplicate entries
  • Automatically triggers Safety Data Sheet search
4

Product Confirmation

Review and confirm product details before adding to your chemical register. Edit information as needed.

  • Review web-scraped product information
  • Edit product name and size before saving
  • Choose between automated data and manual entry
  • Automatic Safety Data Sheet discovery
